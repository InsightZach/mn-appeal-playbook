3. Points for Discussion

The subject's own arm's-length sale governs

The strongest indicator of a property's market value is its own recent open-market sale. 884 Ashland sold for $470,000 on April 10, 2025 — an arm's-length transaction confirmed in the MLS record — less than nine months before the January 2, 2026 effective date. The 2026 EMV of $498,600 sits $28,600 (5.7%) above that purchase price. Absent intervening improvements, the assessment should not exceed what the market itself paid for the property.

Condition is not at issue. Listing photos show an updated, well-maintained interior. This appeal does not claim a condition deduction; it rests on the property's own market sale.
