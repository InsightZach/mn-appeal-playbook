# Assessor Contact Directory

Contacts for opening the open-book conversation and submitting appeals in the example jurisdictions.
**Verify each at the start of every season** — offices, staff, and email addresses change, and the
authoritative contact for a given parcel is always the one printed on its valuation notice.

> Reminder: assessor offices generally discuss parcel-specific appeal detail only with the owner or a
> representative the owner has **authorized in writing**. Have a signed Letter of Authorization on file
> before the first call. (See [Submission](../docs/06-submission.md).)

## Ramsey County — single county-wide authority

Ramsey County assesses every city and township in the county. One office handles all of it.

| Field | Value |
|-------|-------|
| Office | Ramsey County Assessor |
| Email | AskCountyAssessor@ramseycounty.us |
| Phone | 651-266-2131 |
| Appeal path | Open book / informal review → Ramsey County Local Board → Ramsey County Special Board of Appeal & Equalization → MN Tax Court |

## How to find the right office for any other parcel

1. Read the parcel's valuation notice — it names the assessing office and the appeal contact/dates.
2. Or check the county assessor's current list of which cities it assesses vs. which self-assess.

Do not assume county assessment in Hennepin. Confirm per parcel.
